Khoa Nguyen is a fourth-year Statistics and Data Science student at UC Santa Barbara, minoring in Media Arts and Design. His work blends data analytical precision with creative design, exploring the intersections of tecnology, interactivity, and human experience.

He creates interactive systems using coding, electronics, and digital fabrication—ranging from Arduino-based security devices to multimedia installations. Drawing inspiration from everyday life, perception, and cybersecurity, Khoa designs works that invite exploration, spark discussion, and encourage analytical thinking.

Proficient in programming, hardware, and tools like laser cutting and 3D printing, he combines technical skill with a strong sense of visual storytelling to produce pieces that are both functional and engaging.
